**Cabecalho / Metadados (TIDieR Item 1-2):**
- Nome: Protocolo P-01 — Osteoartrite / Artrose (joelho, quadril, coluna, maos)
- Condicao-alvo, populacao (idosos >=60 anos com OA confirmada), setting (centro de reabilitacao geriatrica)
- Alinhamento: OARSI 2019 (Bannuru et al., PubMed 31278997) + ACR 2022. Note: "A revisao de 2024 acrescenta nuances sobre magnitude de efeito, mas nao substitui as recomendacoes de 2019."

**Criterios Diagnosticos e de Inclusao (STRUC-01):**
- ACR classification criteria for knee OA, hip OA, hand OA
- Inclusion criteria: age >=60, confirmed OA diagnosis, able to perform baseline battery
- Exclusion criteria: active inflammatory arthritis (non-OA), unstable cardiac condition, cognitive impairment preventing consent (MMSE <18), acute fracture

**Avaliacao Inicial Mandatoria (ASMNT-01 — reproduced inline from Foundation Layer):**
- TUG: procedure, recording, interpretation with normative values
- SPPB-BR: procedure with Nakano (2007) citation, frailty threshold
- Dinamometria Manual: procedure with EWGSOP2 cutoffs
- Frailty classification using Foundation Layer criteria (Fried + CFS)

**Alinhamento com Diretrizes (EVID-02):**
- OARSI 2019 alignment declaration
- ACR 2022 alignment declaration
- AGS / WHO alignment declaration

**Estrutura de Fases (STRUC-03) — 3 phases:**

Fase 1: Aguda/Protegida
- Entry criteria: post-flare or post-diagnosis baseline, NRS >=4, limited ROM
- Objectives: pain reduction, ROM restoration, patient education
- Typical duration reference: 2-4 weeks (reference only, NOT a progression criterion)
- Functional exit criteria (DOSE-04, ASMNT-03): NRS repouso <=3/10 for 3 consecutive sessions, TUG <14 sec or improvement >=2 sec from baseline, absence of significant joint edema, functional ROM preserved for ADLs

Fase 2: Subaguda/Fortalecimento
- Entry criteria: met all Fase 1 exit criteria
- Objectives: progressive strengthening, endurance, proprioception
- Functional exit criteria: SPPB >=8, grip strength improvement >=MCID, NRS during exercise <=4, able to perform 30-min session without significant fatigue

Fase 3: Avancada/Funcional
- Entry criteria: met all Fase 2 exit criteria
- Objectives: functional integration, sport/leisure return, self-management
- Discharge criteria: WOMAC-BR improvement >=20%, TUG within age-stratified norm, patient demonstrates HEP competence

**Intervencoes por Fase (STRUC-04) — for each phase, classify under:**

1. Modalidade Terapeutica (if justified with evidence):
   - Fase 1: TENS for acute pain (Nivel B, CEBM 2-3), cryotherapy post-session
   - Evidence annotation per intervention (Oxford CEBM format from Foundation Layer)

2. Terapia Manual:
   - Fase 1: joint mobilization Maitland grade I-II (Nivel B)
   - Fase 2: grade III-IV as tolerated (Nivel B)
   - Soft tissue mobilization for periarticular restrictions

3. Cinesioterapia (the core — each with full frailty-stratified dosage tables):
   - Fase 1: isometric exercises (quadriceps, gluteus), ROM exercises, gentle aerobic (walking or aquatic). Dosage table: frailty-stratified, two-column format
   - Fase 2: progressive resistance training (Nivel A, CEBM 1 — Fransen et al. 2015 Cochrane), proprioceptive training, aerobic progression. Full dosage table
   - Fase 3: functional exercises (sit-to-stand, stair climbing, balance challenges), sport-specific training. Full dosage table
   - Aquatic exercise: present as equally valid first-level option per OARSI 2019 (NOT just an alternative for those who cannot tolerate land-based). Include aquatic dosage parameters (per PMC11755622 2025 review)

Each dosage table MUST use the two-column format (Valor Recomendado | Faixa Clinica Aceitavel) with 3 frailty rows (Robusto / Pre-fragil / Fragil) as defined in the Foundation Layer. Parameters: carga inicial, series, repeticoes, frequencia, progressao.

Anti-patterns to avoid:
- Do NOT use time-only progression criteria
- Do NOT prescribe single doses without clinical range
- Do NOT cite evidence level without primary citation
- Do NOT mention outcome tools without -BR suffix and validation citation
- Do NOT present aquatic exercise as a secondary/alternative-only option

**Monitoramento de Dor (SAFE-01):**

  - Fase 2: stop >=6, modify 4-5, acceptable <=4
  - Fase 3: stop >=7, modify 5-6, acceptable <=5
- IASP-aligned post-exercise rule: pain exceeding pre-exercise by >2 NRS points for >24h = overload
- OA-specific note: distinguish inflammatory pain (rest pain, morning stiffness >30min) from mechanical pain (activity-related, relieved by rest) — different clinical implications

**Bandeiras Vermelhas e Escalada (SAFE-02):**
- Red flag table with >=7 entries, OA-specific plus general geriatric:
  - Acute new joint pain without trauma -> reumatologista
  - Sudden joint swelling with warmth/redness -> urgencia (septic arthritis rule-out)
  - Fever with joint pain -> urgencia
  - Radicular pain with new neurological deficit -> neurocirurgia
  - Progressive rest/night pain (oncological?) -> oncologista
  - Disproportionate dyspnea -> cardiologista
  - Symptomatic orthostatic hypotension (>20mmHg systolic drop) -> clinico
- Escalation pathway format: sign/symptom | action | destination

**Interacoes com Polifarmacia (SAFE-03):**
- Reproduce polypharmacy table from Foundation Layer, customized for OA context:
  - AINEs: pain masking -> reinforce NRS monitoring; avoid high-intensity unsupervised
  - Corticosteroides (intra-articular or systemic chronic): myopathy, bone loss -> reduce impact loads
  - Anti-hipertensivos (betabloqueadores): blunted HR -> use RPE not HR targets
  - Anti-hipertensivos (vasodilatadores/diureticos): orthostatic hypotension -> mandatory cool-down, slow position changes
  - Anticoagulantes: bleeding risk -> avoid contact exercises, monitor bruising
  - Benzodiazepinicos: drowsiness, fall risk -> schedule away from peak action, emphasize balance supervision
- AGS Beers 2023 reference with "verificar atualizacoes" note

**Precaucoes Cardiorrespiratorias (SAFE-04):**
- Pre-participation cardiovascular assessment documentation requirement
- Immediate stop indicators (chest pain, disproportionate dyspnea, palpitations with dizziness, SBP >200 or drop >10 during progressive effort, SpO2 <90%)
- ICC/DPOC known: start at Fragil tier regardless of frailty score, shorter sessions (15-20min), RPE <=13

**Indicadores de Desfecho (ASMNT-02, ASMNT-03, ASMNT-04):**
- Primary outcomes with re-evaluation schedule:
  - WOMAC-BR (Oliveira et al. 2022, PMC9673866): baseline + every phase transition + discharge. Target: >=20% improvement
  - SPPB-BR (Nakano 2007): baseline + every phase transition. MCID: 0.5-1.0 points
  - TUG: baseline + every phase transition. Target: within age-stratified norm or improvement >=2 sec
  - Dinamometria Manual: baseline + discharge. MCID: M 5.0-6.5 kg, F 2.7-5.0 kg
- Re-evaluation schedule table: tool | frequency | decision threshold
