**P-15 DPOC (target: 750-900 lines, 15 sections):**

Section 1 — Criterios de Inclusao/Exclusao:
- DPOC confirmada (espirometria FEV1/FVC < 0.7 pos-broncodilatador)
- GOLD II+ com sintomas (mMRC >= 2 ou CAT >= 10)
- Estabilidade clinica (sem exacerbacao nos ultimos 28 dias)
- NÃO COBRE block: asma nao-DPOC, exacerbacao aguda, IC como diagnostico primario (→ P-16), hipertensao pulmonar severa (PAP > 55 mmHg)
- Fenotipo de Fried como classificador de fragilidade (Robusto/Pre-fragil/Fragil)

Section 2 — Bateria de Avaliacao:
- Universal battery (TUG, SPPB-BR, Dinamometria Manual) from SHARED-FOUNDATION
- Condition-specific: TC6M (ATS 2002 statement procedural; Britto 2013 BR reference equation with note on geriatric limitation), mMRC, CAT, Borg Dispneia 0-10, SpO2 basal
- Verificar citacoes BR para CAT (buscar durante pesquisa; se nao confirmada, usar versao GOLD com nota de cautela)

Section 3 — Classificacao e Estratificacao:
- GOLD ABCD staging (FEV1 + mMRC/CAT) per GOLD 2025
- Frailty overlay: Fried phenotype x GOLD stage matrix for dosage adjustment

Sections 4-7 — Fases terapeuticas (3 fases clinicas):
- Phase structure: Fase 1 Aguda/Protegida, Fase 2 Subaguda/Fortalecimento, Fase 3 Avancada/Funcional
- Each phase: entry/exit criteria (function-based, not time-based), dosage tables (two-column: recommended + acceptable range), frailty-stratified (Robust/Pre-frail/Frail)
- Aerobic intensity via Borg Dispneia 0-10: target 4-6 (moderate dyspnea); Frail start 3-4
- Mandatory: breathing exercises (pursed-lip, diaphragmatic), peripheral muscle training, functional training
- TC6M distance targets per phase with MCID (25-30 m) as progression threshold
- Reference P-08 Section 6.3 for resistance dosing (do NOT re-derive)
- Reference P-07 Section 5.2 for balance hierarchy (do NOT re-derive)

Section 8 — Manejo de Dor:
- NRS thresholds per IASP alignment (from SHARED-FOUNDATION)
- Musculoskeletal pain common in DPOC (deconditioning, osteoporosis comorbidity)

Section 9 — Sinais de Alerta e Escalacao:
- Red flag table with escalation pathways
- Exacerbation recognition (increased sputum, color change, worsening dyspnea)
- When to suspend and refer

Section 10 — Interacoes Farmacologicas:
- Broncodilatadores (SABA, LABA, LAMA): timing relative to exercise session
- Corticosteroides inalatorios: osteoporosis risk → cross-reference P-04
- Corticosteroides sistemicos: sarcopenia/miopatia risk → cross-reference P-08
- Oxigenoterapia domiciliar: titration during exercise if SpO2 < 90%

Section 11 — Precaucoes Cardiorrespiratorias:
- THIS IS THE UNIVERSAL TEMPLATE SECTION — design it for reuse across all 20 protocols
- 11.1: Protocolo de Avaliacao Pre-Sessao (UNIVERSAL) — PA, FC, SpO2, dispneia em repouso, edema (per Research template)
- 11.2: Indicadores de Parada Imediata — table with all thresholds (per Research template)
- 11.3: Monitoramento Durante a Sessao — SpO2 continuous/intermittent (every 5 min in DPOC), Borg dispneia every 10 min
- 11.4: Adaptacoes por Comorbidade Cardiorrespiratoria coexistente
- 11.5: Especificidades P-15 DPOC — dessaturacao silenciosa, O2 supplementation protocol during exercise

Section 12 — Indicadores de Desfecho:
- Primary: TC6M (MCID 25-30 m), Britto 2013 BR reference
- Secondary: mMRC, CAT (MCID >= 2 points), Borg Dispneia pico
- Reassessment schedule: 4 weeks, 8 weeks, 12 weeks (discharge)

Section 13 — Interacao de Comorbidades:
- Cross-reference P-08 (sarcopenia — high prevalence in DPOC), P-04 (osteoporosis — corticosteroid use), P-07 (falls — deconditioning + balance), P-01 (OA — concurrent mobility limitation)

Section 14 — Programa Domiciliar (HEP):
- Walking program with SpO2 self-monitoring (if patient has home oximeter)
- Breathing exercises daily
- Frailty-adapted complexity

Section 15 — Validacao Clinica:
- 8 clinical validity questions per template established in P-01 Section 15

**P-16 INSUFICIENCIA CARDIACA (target: 750-900 lines, 15 sections):**

Section 1 — Criterios de Inclusao/Exclusao:
- IC confirmada (diagnostico cardiologico documentado)
- NYHA I-III estavel (IV com compensacao recente: mobilizacao leve supervisionada apenas)
- Differentiate IC-FEr (FEVE <= 40%) vs IC-FEp (FEVE >= 50%) per AHA 2023
- NÃO COBRE block: IC aguda descompensada, NYHA IV instavel, arritmia ventricular nao controlada, angina instavel, DPOC como diagnostico primario (→ P-15)

Section 2 — Bateria de Avaliacao:
- Universal battery from SHARED-FOUNDATION
- Condition-specific: NYHA classification, MLHFQ (cite BR version with validation note per research open questions), RPE Borg 6-20 basal, PA, FC repouso, peso corporal basal, edema assessment
- TC6M as secondary outcome (primary in DPOC, secondary in IC)

Sections 4-7 — Fases terapeuticas:
- RPE Borg 6-20 EXCLUSIVELY for intensity (target 11-14 moderate; Frail/NYHA III: 10-11 max 12)
- EXPLICIT DECLARATION: "FC como parametro de intensidade e CONTRAINDICADO em pacientes com betabloqueador. Usar RPE Borg 6-20 exclusivamente." — in bold, in dosage table header, and in Section 10
- IC-FEr vs IC-FEp: same structure, FEp uses AHA 2023 Scientific Statement (CIR.0000000000001122) for dosage rationale
- Reference P-08 Section 6.3 for resistance dosing
- Reference P-07 Section 5.2 for balance hierarchy

Section 10 — Interacoes Farmacologicas:
- Betabloqueadores: FC embotada — RPE only (detail mechanism)
- Diureticos: desidratacao, hipotensao ortostatica, eletrólitos
- IECA/BRA: hipotensao pos-exercicio
- Digoxina: intoxicacao sinais
- Anticoagulantes: risco de sangramento em quedas

Section 11 — Precaucoes Cardiorrespiratorias:
- Inherit universal template from P-15 Section 11 (11.1 + 11.2 identical)
- 11.3: Monitoramento Durante Sessao — RPE every 10 min (NOT FC), PA if symptomatic
- 11.4: Comorbidade DPOC coexistente adaptations
- 11.5: Especificidades P-16 IC:
  - Daily weight monitoring protocol (instruction, thresholds >= 2 kg/3 days, >= 1.5 kg/24h, cardiology pathway) per Research template
  - Thermoregulation section: ambient temp <= 22C ideal, max 26C, suspend if > 30C without AC, pre-session hydration 200 mL 30 min before (if no fluid restriction), mechanism explanation (AHA Circulation 2006)
  - Edema monitoring protocol

Section 12 — Indicadores de Desfecho:
- Primary: MLHFQ (MCID >= 5 points)
- Secondary: TC6M, NYHA class change, RPE at submaximal workload
- Reassessment: 4 weeks, 8 weeks, 12 weeks

Section 15 — 8 clinical validity questions per template

**Anti-patterns to avoid (from Research):**
- DO NOT use FC as intensity parameter in P-16
- DO NOT confuse Borg RPE (6-20) with Borg Dispneia (0-10) — DPOC uses dispneia, IC uses RPE
- DO NOT omit SpO2 monitoring in P-15
- DO NOT ignore daily weight monitoring in P-16
- DO NOT classify NYHA IV as absolute contraindication without nuance
- DO NOT omit IC-FEr vs IC-FEp differentiation
