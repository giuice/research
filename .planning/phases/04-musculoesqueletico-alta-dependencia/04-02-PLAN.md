**STEP 0 — Differentiation Analysis (perform BEFORE any drafting):**

Establish the scope boundary between P-10 and P-11 using the pattern from the research document. Each protocol MUST open with a scope statement block:

P-10 scope: Reabilitacao pos-operatoria de artroplastia total de joelho (ATJ). Nao cobre: artroplastia de quadril (ver P-11), fratura periprotetica de joelho (encaminhar ortopedia), artroplastia de revisao de joelho (necessita adaptacao). Riscos especificos: rigidez articular (deficit de flexao/extensao), sindrome compartimental pos-op, deficit de controle neuromuscular do quadriceps, TVP pos-op.

P-11 scope: Reabilitacao pos-operatoria de artroplastia total de quadril (ATQ) por qualquer abordagem cirurgica (posterior, anterolateral, anterior direto). Nao cobre: fratura periprotetica de quadril (encaminhar ortopedia), artroplastia de joelho (ver P-10), hemiartroplastia de quadril por fratura (ver P-05). Riscos especificos: luxacao protetica (abordagem-dependente), deficit de abdutores, discrepancia de membros inferiores, TVP pos-op.

**STEP 1 — P-10 ATJ Protocol (15 sections):**

**Section 1 — Criterios Diagnosticos e de Inclusao:**
- OA criteria (coxartrose terminal ou artrite) + indicacao cirurgica + estado pos-op
- Include scope statement block (from Step 0)
- Inclusion: age >= 60, post-primary TKA, admitted to rehabilitation
- Exclusion: revision TKA (requires adaptation), periprosthetic fracture, active infection, tumor prosthesis

**Section 2 — Avaliacao Inicial Mandatoria:**
- TUG + SPPB-BR + Dinamometria (inherited SHARED-FOUNDATION)
- Active ROM of knee: flexion (target >= 90 degrees by 6 weeks, >= 120 by 12 weeks) and extension (target 0 degrees — full extension deficit is a primary ATJ concern)
- BBS-BR + FES-I-BR (post-arthroplasty fall risk)
- WOMAC-BR (Oliveira 2022, PMC9673866) — primary functional outcome for P-10
- CFS if not recently assessed (follow P-05 pattern but not mandatory at admission for elective ATJ)
- Quadriceps lag assessment: inability to achieve final 15 degrees of active extension against gravity

**Section 3 — Alinhamento com Diretrizes:**
- AAOS CPG Surgical Management OA of the Knee 2021
- Cite systematic reviews for TKA rehabilitation (PMC6829007)
- Oxford CEBM 2011 evidence grading (inherited)

**Section 4 — Estrutura de Fases:** 3 phases:
- Fase 1: Aguda/Protegida (0-6 semanas pos-op)
- Fase 2: Subaguda/Fortalecimento (6-12 semanas)
- Fase 3: Avancada/Funcional (> 12 semanas)
Temporal references = minimum safety windows; progression = function-based.

**Section 5 — Fase 1 (Aguda/Protegida):**
- WBAT Day 1 post-op (ERAS protocol)
- Cryotherapy for edema management (Nivel C)
- ROM exercises: passive then active-assisted flexion/extension
- Quadriceps activation: isometrics, straight leg raise, active extension
- Patellar mobilization
- Gait training with walker
- Apply P-07 Session Fall Risk Protocol (cite P-07 Secao 5.2)
- Two-column dosage tables with 3 frailty rows
- Exit criteria: active flexion >= 90 degrees, active extension deficit <= 5 degrees, independent transfers, ambulation >= 30m with device

**Section 6 — Fase 2 (Subaguda/Fortalecimento):**
- Progressive resistance training — cite P-08 Secao 6.3
- ROM progression toward >= 120 degrees flexion
- Functional training: stairs, sit-to-stand, low step-ups
- Dynamic balance (Level 2 P-07 hierarchy)
- Proprioceptive training
- Bone-loading constraints if T-score documented <= -2.5 (cite P-04 Secao 5.2)
- Two-column dosage tables with 3 frailty rows
- Exit criteria: active flexion >= 110 degrees, full extension, TUG < 15 sec, independent stairs with rail, SPPB >= 8

**Section 7 — Fase 3 (Avancada/Funcional):**
- Advanced functional training
- Dual-task and perturbation if criteria met (P-07 hierarchy Levels 3-4)
- Sport-specific or recreational activity adaptation (as appropriate for geriatric population)
- HEP establishment
- Two-column dosage tables with 3 frailty rows
- Discharge criteria: active flexion >= 120 degrees, full extension, TUG < 13.5 sec, SPPB >= 10, BBS >= 45, WOMAC-BR improvement >= MCID

**Section 8 — Monitoramento de Dor:** NRS thresholds (inherited template). Differentiate surgical site pain from new joint pain.

**Section 9 — Bandeiras Vermelhas:**
- All general red flags (SHARED-FOUNDATION Secao 7.2)
- P-10 SPECIFIC — Sindrome Compartimental Pos-ATJ (use exact Code Example from research):
  - 5 Ps: Pain (dor desproporcional ao movimento passivo — dorsiflexao passiva de tornozelo), Pressure, Pallor, Paresthesia, Paralysis
  - Warning: epidural block may mask pain signal (PubMed 16498006)
  - Rule: any 2+ signs = suspend immediately + orthopedic emergency
- Prosthetic infection (erythema, warmth, drainage, fever, CRP/ESR elevation)
- Severe stiffness (arthrofibrosis — flexion < 70 degrees at 6 weeks despite compliance)
- DVT symptoms

**Section 10 — Polifarmacia:** 6 SHARED-FOUNDATION base + anticoagulantes (TEV pos-op — follow P-05 pattern for scope of practice declaration), NSAIDs (heterotopic ossification prophylaxis concern vs. fracture healing — document awareness), opioides (post-op pain management and exercise timing).

**Section 11 — Precaucoes Cardiorrespiratorias:** Inherited template.

**Section 12 — Indicadores de Desfecho:** TUG, SPPB-BR, BBS-BR, FES-I-BR, WOMAC-BR (primary), active knee ROM (flexion + extension), quadriceps strength (isometric or isokinetic when available). Re-evaluation schedule.

**Section 13 — Rastreabilidade e Flags:** Cross-reference P-04 Secao 5.2, P-08 Secao 6.3, P-07 Secoes 5.2/7.2. Reference P-05 for fracture-related context. Reference P-11 for ATQ differentiation.

**Section 14 — Referencias por Nivel de Evidencia:** All citations organized by CEBM level.

**STEP 2 — P-11 ATQ Protocol (15 sections):**

**Section 1 — Criterios Diagnosticos e de Inclusao:**
- Coxartrose terminal + indicacao cirurgica + estado pos-op
- Include scope statement block (from Step 0)
- Mandatory: document surgical approach (posterior / anterolateral / anterior direto) BEFORE starting rehab
- Inclusion: age >= 60, post-primary THA by any approach, admitted to rehabilitation
- Exclusion: revision THA, periprosthetic fracture, hemiarthroplasty for fracture (see P-05), tumor prosthesis

**Section 2 — Avaliacao Inicial Mandatoria:**
- TUG + SPPB-BR + Dinamometria (inherited SHARED-FOUNDATION)
- Active ROM of hip: flexion, extension, abduction, rotation — assess per approach restrictions
- BBS-BR + FES-I-BR (post-arthroplasty fall risk)
- WOMAC-BR (primary functional outcome)
- Abductor strength assessment (manual or dynamometry) — critical for Trendelenburg evaluation
- Limb length discrepancy measurement (if reported by patient)

**Section 3 — Alinhamento com Diretrizes:**
- AAOS CPG Total Hip Arthroplasty
- PMC9464790 systematic review THA rehabilitation
- Oxford CEBM 2011 (inherited)

**Section 4 — Estrutura de Fases:** Same 3-phase structure with temporal minimums + functional criteria.

**Section 5 — Fase 1 (Aguda/Protegida):**
- WBAT Day 1 (ERAS)
- **APPROACH-SPECIFIC PRECAUTIONS (use exact Code Example from research):**
  - Abordagem Posterior: classic precautions (flexion > 90, adduction, internal rotation) + 2024 meta-analysis evidence (PMC11651519) showing no difference in dislocation rate with/without precautions + declared policy: follow surgeon orientation, document
  - Abordagem Anterior Direta: avoid extension beyond neutral, excessive external rotation, prone sleeping
  - Abordagem Anterolateral: minimal precautions, monitor abductor deficit (superior gluteal nerve risk)
- Instruction: "Verificar com o cirurgiao responsavel o tipo de abordagem utilizada e registrar no prontuario ANTES de iniciar qualquer exercicio de amplitude de movimento de quadril"
- Hip abductor activation (isometrics initially, progressing per approach)
- Gait training with walker
- Apply P-07 Session Fall Risk Protocol (cite P-07 Secao 5.2)
- Two-column dosage tables with 3 frailty rows
- Exit criteria: independent transfers with precaution compliance, ambulation >= 30m with device, active hip flexion >= 70 degrees (approach permitting)

**Section 6 — Fase 2 (Subaguda/Fortalecimento):**
- Progressive resistance — cite P-08 Secao 6.3
- Abductor strengthening progression (open chain → closed chain → functional)
- Gait normalization (eliminate Trendelenburg pattern)
- Dynamic balance (Level 2 P-07 hierarchy)
- Precaution relaxation timeline (typically post-6 weeks, per surgeon clearance — do NOT auto-relax)
- Bone-loading constraints if T-score <= -2.5 (cite P-04 Secao 5.2)
- Two-column dosage tables with 3 frailty rows
- Exit criteria: no Trendelenburg gait, TUG < 15 sec, independent stairs, SPPB >= 8

**Section 7 — Fase 3 (Avancada/Funcional):**
- Advanced functional and community mobility training
- Dual-task and perturbation if criteria met (P-07 hierarchy Levels 3-4)
- HEP establishment with long-term hip strength maintenance
- Two-column dosage tables with 3 frailty rows
- Discharge criteria: TUG < 13.5 sec, SPPB >= 10, BBS >= 45, no Trendelenburg, WOMAC-BR improvement >= MCID

**Section 8 — Monitoramento de Dor:** NRS thresholds (inherited). Differentiate surgical pain, referred groin pain, and new pain suggesting dislocation.

**Section 9 — Bandeiras Vermelhas:**
- General (SHARED-FOUNDATION Secao 7.2)
- P-11 SPECIFIC: Luxacao protetica (acute sharp pain + involuntary internal rotation + leg length discrepancy + inability to bear weight — emergency), prosthetic infection, periprosthetic fracture (sudden inability to weight bear + new thigh pain), DVT symptoms, persistent Trendelenburg > 12 weeks (possible nerve injury)

**Section 10 — Polifarmacia:** 6 SHARED-FOUNDATION base + anticoagulantes (TEV pos-op, scope of practice declaration consistent with P-05 pattern), NSAIDs, opioides.

**Section 11 — Precaucoes Cardiorrespiratorias:** Inherited template.

**Section 12 — Indicadores de Desfecho:** TUG, SPPB-BR, BBS-BR, FES-I-BR, WOMAC-BR (primary), hip ROM, abductor strength, Trendelenburg assessment. Re-evaluation schedule.

**Section 13 — Rastreabilidade e Flags:** Cross-reference P-04 Secao 5.2, P-08 Secao 6.3, P-07 Secoes 5.2/7.2. Reference P-05 for fracture context. Reference P-10 for ATJ differentiation.

**Section 14 — Referencias por Nivel de Evidencia:** All citations organized by CEBM level. Include PMC11651519 (precaution meta-analysis), PMC9464790, PMC10087740.

**Section 15 — As 8 Questoes de Validade Clinica:** All 8 answered.

- Do NOT omit compartment syndrome from P-10 red flags (Pitfall 4) — include 5 Ps + epidural masking warning
