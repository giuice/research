- Universal battery (TUG, SPPB-BR, Dynamometry) — inherited

**Seção 3: Alinhamento com Diretrizes**
- Cochrane PFMT (Dumoulin 2018), ICS/IUGA 2010, ABRAFISM 2025, SciELO PT systematic review

**Seção 4: Estrutura de Fases** — 3 phases with DIVERGENT pathways:
- Phase structure splits by subtype: Seção 4a (Hipotônico/PFMT), Seção 4b (Hipertônico/Down-Training), Seção 4c (Misto/Sequencial)
- Function-based entry/exit criteria per subtype

**Seções 5-7: Phases 1-3 detailed** with frailty-stratified dosage tables (Robusto/Pré-frágil/Frágil per Fried phenotype). Must include BOTH therapeutic tracks:

For HYPOTONIC track:
- Phase 1: Kegel basics (isolate contraction, 3-5 sec holds, biofeedback activation, electrostimulation if Oxford ≤ 2)
- Phase 2: Progressive PFMT (longer holds, more reps, fast-twitch fiber training, The Knack maneuver)
- Phase 3: Functional integration (PFMT during ADLs, maintenance program, community exercise)

For HYPERTONIC track:
- Phase 1: Down-training (diaphragmatic breathing + perineal awareness, progressive relaxation, inhibition biofeedback, internal myofascial release, NEVER Kegel)
- Phase 2: Controlled re-activation (gentle contractions with full relaxation emphasis, coordination training)
- Phase 3: Functional integration (maintaining relaxation during activity, pain-free movement patterns)

Two-column dosage format throughout. Objective progression criteria (never time-based alone).

**Seção 8: Monitoramento de Dor** — EVA for pelvic pain (critical in hypertonic); NRS with adaptations

**Seção 9: Bandeiras Vermelhas e Escalonamento** — Hematuria, new-onset incontinence with neurological signs, pelvic mass, fecal incontinence new-onset, worsening despite 12 weeks of correct treatment

**Seção 10: Interações com Polifarmácia** — Anticolinérgicos (xerostomia, constipação), diuréticos (aumento volume urinário), alfa-bloqueadores (hipotensão), antidepressivos (efeitos sobre tônus vesical), HRT (efeito sobre mucosa vaginal)

**Seção 11: Precauções Cardiorrespiratórias** — Universal template VERBATIM from SHARED-FOUNDATION Seção 7.4

**Seção 12: Indicadores de Desfecho** — Primary: ICIQ-SF-BR (MCID >= 3 points with confidence note). Secondary: Modified Oxford Scale change, PERFECT Scheme improvement, pad test, FES-I-BR. Re-evaluation schedule (every 4 weeks).
