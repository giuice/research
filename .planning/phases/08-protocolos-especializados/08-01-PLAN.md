- ADA 2025 Standards Section 12, Colberg 2016 Position Statement, ADA 2017 Diabetic Neuropathy Position Statement

**Seção 4: Estrutura de Fases** — 3 phases (Aguda/Protegida, Subaguda/Fortalecimento, Avançada/Funcional)

**Seções 5-7: Phases 1-3 detailed** with frailty-stratified dosage tables (Robusto/Pré-frágil/Frágil per Fried phenotype). Include:
- Sensory re-education hierarchy SE-1 through SE-4 mapped to phases (SE-1 in Phase 1, SE-2 in Phase 2, SE-3-4 in Phase 3)

- Foot inspection procedure BEFORE each session (2-min visual inspection by PT)
- Calçado: ALWAYS required — NEVER barefoot exercise in NPD
- Two-column dosage format (recommended value + acceptable clinical range)
- Objective progression criteria (never time-based alone)

**Seção 8: Monitoramento de Dor** — NRS with verbal anchors when MCI present; neuropathic pain specific considerations

**Seção 9: Bandeiras Vermelhas e Escalonamento** — NPD-specific red flags table per 08-RESEARCH.md (ulcer, hypoglycemia, ischemia, foot drop, autonomic instability)

**Seção 10: Interações com Polifarmácia** — 7+ drug classes: insulin/sulfonylureas (hypoglycemia risk), metformin (B12 deficiency → neuropathy), gabapentin/pregabalin (dizziness/sedation), duloxetine (nausea/dizziness), antihypertensives (orthostatic hypotension), statins (myalgia), opioids (if co-prescribed)

**Seção 11: Precauções Cardiorrespiratórias** — Universal template VERBATIM from SHARED-FOUNDATION Seção 7.4 (Sections 11.1 + 11.2). Add NPD-specific: autonomic neuropathy may blunt HR response and mask cardiac symptoms — RPE Borg 6-20 as primary intensity monitor (same rationale as P-16 IC with beta-blockers)

**Seção 12: Indicadores de Desfecho** — Primary: BBS-BR, SPPB-BR, TUG. Secondary: MNSI score change, FES-I-BR, monofilament mapping progression. Re-evaluation schedule.

**Seção 13: Rastreabilidade e Flags de Comorbidade** — Cross-references to P-07 (MANDATORY), P-08 (when sarcopenia), P-04 (when osteoporosis), P-01 (when OA coexists)

**Seção 14: Referências por Nível de Evidência** — Oxford CEBM classification. Include PMIDs for all citations.

**Seção 15: As 8 Questões de Validade Clínica** — Answer all 8 questions unambiguously (who qualifies, when to stop, where to start, what to do, how much, how fast, how to measure progress, why these choices)
